I like to make things.

Whether it's a sweet tune, creative writing, or a beautiful website, I live to express myself.

My first interaction with coding came by way of Myspace in the mid 2000's. I really didn't know much about coding, but I would copy and paste templates and then dig through the code and adjust different things to see if they would do what I wanted them to do on my profile. I spent the next several years working normal jobs and hating it, until one day, I decided if I wanted to break free from a traditional career, I needed to do something different than I was doing.

So, in 2013 I enrolled in Treehouse, an online code school. I took a short break to focus on planning my wedding, and then I started a more in-depth code bootcamp through CareerFoundry. During that course, I learned mostly HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I also worked with Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Ajax, Angular, caching strategy, JQuery, Git, Heroku, and frameworks such as Bootstrap.

Since then, I have been doing freelance work and building my portfolio up. If you have any questions about my journey or would like to know more about me, be sure to drop a line in my contact section. I'd love to chat!
